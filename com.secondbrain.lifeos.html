Second Brain Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 29, 2026 · Last updated: June 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Second Brain ("we", "us", "the App") collects, how it's used, and your rights. Second Brain is published by Brad Goldenberg. Contact: edwardgoldenberg@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

Information stored on your device only

Second Brain stores all of your personal content — health logs, expenses, tasks, habits, notes, and journal entries — locally on your device. This information is not transmitted to our servers and is not visible to us. Backups are handled by iCloud (if enabled by you in iOS Settings) under Apple's terms.

Identifiers used for advertising

Second Brain uses Google AdMob to display occasional ads to users who choose to watch a rewarded ad in lieu of upgrading to Pro. To deliver ads, AdMob may collect your device's advertising identifier (IDFA), a coarse approximation of your location derived from your IP address, and ad-interaction data (which ads you've seen and tapped). On iOS, the App will request your permission via the system "App Tracking Transparency" prompt before this identifier is used to track you across apps; if you decline, only non-personalized contextual ads are shown. The Google Mobile Ads SDK also collects device performance data (app launch time, hang rate, energy usage) and crash logs to ensure ads render correctly. See Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob's data disclosure document for full details on AdMob's data practices.

Purchases

In-app purchases (Pro Monthly and Pro Annual subscriptions) are processed by the Apple App Store. We do not see or store your payment details. Receipts are validated through Apple's StoreKit APIs.

Apple Health (optional)

If you grant Second Brain permission to read Apple Health data, that data is read on-device only and used to populate your Health dashboard. We do not transmit Apple Health data off your device.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's local functionality (your data stays on device).
  • To display advertisements via Google AdMob (the only third-party advertising provider used).
  • To process in-app purchases via Apple's StoreKit.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone. We share data only with the third-party services listed above:

  • Google AdMob — advertising identifier for ad targeting and measurement (when ads are shown).
  • Apple — receipt validation for in-app purchases and (if enabled) iCloud backup.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR in Europe, CCPA in California, similar laws elsewhere) you may have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you (which for Second Brain is "none" — everything is on your device).
  • Erase all local data using the "Erase all data" button in Settings → Danger zone.
  • Opt out of personalized advertising via iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, or by declining the App Tracking Transparency prompt.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at edwardgoldenberg@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Second Brain is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Data Retention

All of your content is retained on your device until you delete it (per entry or via Settings → Erase all data). We do not retain any data on our servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
