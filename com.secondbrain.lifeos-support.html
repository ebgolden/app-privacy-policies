FAQ

  • Do I need an account? No. All data stays on your device.
  • How do I cancel my Pro subscription? Go to iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Second Brain.
  • How do I erase my data? Settings → Danger zone → Erase all data.
  • Privacy? See our Privacy Policy.
