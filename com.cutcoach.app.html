CutCoach Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 31, 2026 - Last updated: May 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information CutCoach ("we", "us", "the App") collects, how it's used, and your rights. CutCoach is published by Edward Goldenberg. Contact: edwardgoldenberg@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

Identifiers used for advertising

CutCoach uses Google AdMob to display ads in the free version of the App. To deliver personalized ads, AdMob may collect your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android), a coarse approximation of your location derived from your IP address, and ad-interaction data (which ads you've seen and tapped). On iOS, the App will request your permission via the system "App Tracking Transparency" prompt before this identifier is used to track you across apps; if you decline, only non-personalized contextual ads are shown. The Google Mobile Ads SDK also collects device performance data (app launch time, hang rate, energy usage) and crash logs to ensure ads render correctly. See Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob's data disclosure document for full details on AdMob's data practices.

Purchases

In-app purchases (CutCoach Premium subscriptions and the one-time Deep Report) are processed by Apple App Store. We do not see or store your payment details. Receipts are validated through Apple's StoreKit APIs. We use react-native-iap to interact with StoreKit.

Camera and Photos

The App requests access to your camera and photo library only when you tap a feature that needs them - specifically, when you take or pick a selfie for hair analysis. The captured or selected photo is used solely to generate your hair analysis and hairstyle try-ons.

Server-side data

The App sends the single selfie you choose to Google's Gemini API (gemini-3-pro-preview) for on-demand hair analysis and to generate personalized hairstyle try-on images. The photo is transmitted over HTTPS, processed by Google, and the resulting analysis and images are returned to your device. Google's handling of data sent to the Gemini API is governed by the Gemini API Terms of Service and Google's Privacy Policy. We do not operate our own servers - your photo is not stored by us. Analysis history is kept locally on your device only.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's hair-analysis and try-on functionality.
  • To display advertisements via Google AdMob in the free version (the only third-party advertising provider used).
  • To process subscription and one-time purchases via Apple's StoreKit.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone. We share data only with the third-party services listed above (each linked to its own privacy policy):

  • Google AdMob - advertising identifier for ad targeting and measurement.
  • Google Gemini API - your selfie image for hair analysis and try-on generation.
  • Apple StoreKit - in-app purchase processing.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR in Europe, CCPA in California, similar laws elsewhere) you may have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your data. (Analysis history can be cleared from Settings > Data & History inside the App.)
  • Opt out of tracking or personalized advertising (use iOS "Limit Ad Tracking" or Android "Reset advertising ID" / "Opt out of Ads Personalization").

To exercise any of these rights, email us at edwardgoldenberg@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

CutCoach is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Data Retention

Your selfies are not retained by us - they are sent to the Gemini API on-demand and the results are returned to your device. Your analysis history is stored locally on your device only and can be cleared at any time from Settings > Data & History. Google may retain data sent to the Gemini API per its own retention policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
