SubWatch Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 May 2026

SubWatch ("the App", "we", "us") is published by Edward Goldenberg. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App handles and how. SubWatch is designed to keep your financial data on your device.

Data we collect from you

None. SubWatch does not require any account, login, or sign-up. The App does not transmit your transaction data, subscription list, or fraud alerts to us, our servers, or any third party. All transaction processing happens locally on your device using on-device SQLite storage.

Data the App processes locally on your device

  • Transaction CSV files you import. When you choose a CSV file from your device, the App parses it locally and stores the resulting transactions in an on-device SQLite database. This data never leaves your device.
  • Manually entered transactions. Same handling as imported transactions — local only.
  • Subscription, duplicate, and fraud detection results. Computed on-device from your transactions and stored locally.

Data collected by Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

The App displays banner and occasional interstitial advertisements through the Google Mobile Ads SDK. The SDK is provided by Google LLC and may collect:

  • Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA). Used to deliver and personalize ads. We request your permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt before tracking. If you choose "Ask App Not to Track", IDFA access is disabled and ads will be non-personalized.
  • Device information. Generic information about your device model, OS version, and app version is collected by the Mobile Ads SDK for ad delivery and reporting.
  • Ad interaction data. Information about which ads you view or tap, used for advertising attribution.

This data is collected, used, and shared by Google in accordance with Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy. We do not receive or store this data.

Tracking

The Google Mobile Ads SDK uses the IDFA to track activity for advertising purposes when you grant tracking permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt. You may revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

In-app purchases

The App contains no active in-app purchases in v1.0. Future versions may offer a "Remove Ads" upgrade through Apple's standard StoreKit. Any future purchases will be processed by Apple — we never see your payment details.

Children's privacy

SubWatch is not directed to children under 13. The App does not knowingly collect data from children.

Data retention and deletion

Because all your data is stored locally on your device, you control retention. You can delete all SubWatch data at any time via Settings → Clear Data in the App, or by uninstalling the App. There is no server-side data to delete.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, the "Last updated" date above will be revised. Continued use of the App after a change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
