Frequently Asked Questions

The app says no bars are nearby. What gives?

PintPath only shows bars within a half-mile walk of where you're standing. Make sure (1) you've granted Location permission in iOS Settings → Privacy → Location Services, and (2) you're actually somewhere with bars within a half mile. Try the map view to see how far out we're looking.

The prices at a bar are wrong.

Prices come from happy-hour menus and verified user reports. If you spot a stale price, tap the venue and use "Report price" — we'll update the listing.

How do I cancel my PintPath Pro subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Go to iOS Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → PintPath Pro → Cancel.

How do I delete the app and my data?

Long-press the PintPath icon on your home screen and tap "Remove App" → "Delete App". Because we don't keep user accounts on our servers, this fully removes all your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I add a bar that's missing?

Yes — email us the bar's name and address and we'll add it.
